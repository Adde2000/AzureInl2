Asparagus — A Modern Ode

Tender green spears, savory notes, and a springtime crunch — why asparagus belongs on every great plate.

Crunch & Tenderness

Blanched or grilled, asparagus keeps a satisfying bite while staying delicate and juicy.

Fresh Green Flavor

Bright, grassy notes with subtle sweetness — it tastes like spring itself.

Umami Depth

When roasted, asparagus develops a nutty, savory complexity that pairs perfectly with butter, lemon, and Parmesan.

Quick Cooking Tips

  • Snap off the woody ends.
  • Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 8–10 minutes.
  • Top with lemon juice, olive oil, or shaved cheese.

Perfect Pairings

Eggs, seafood, nutty cheeses, and crisp white wines make asparagus shine.
